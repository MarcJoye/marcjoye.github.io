Cancellation policy

The registration fee can be refunded if you notify us of your cancellation through the Pairing 2010 Registration website before or on November 27, 2010. You will receive 100% refund except for remittance charge. There will be no refund after November 27, 2010.

Stipends

A limited number of stipends and a reduced registration fee are available to those unable to obtain funding to attend the conference, and to students whose papers are accepted and who will present the paper themselves. Requests for stipends should be addressed to the general chair until August 26.

Stipend application deadline

Application page available from: August 1, 2010
Application deadline: August 26, 2010
Notification of successful applicants: September 10, 2010

How to apply for stipends

  1. Complete the online grant application form. Your Supporting Statement is the most important part of your application: it should describe your current research projects and how the conference will relate with it and be helpful and claim how your paper is attractive to Pairing 2010 attendees.
  2. Do make conference reservations early.
  3. Send your student ID: fax it to +81-3-5298-4522 or send a hard copy to

    Pairing 2010 Organization Committee
    Attn: (Dr) Akira Otsuka, General Chair
    c/o RCIS, AIST
    Akihabara Daibiru #1003,
    1-18-13 Sotokanda Chiyoda-ku,
    101-0021 Tokyo JAPAN.

    Please state on the fax or on the head of the letter that "a grant applicant to Pairing 2010".
  4. All materials must arrive at Pairing 2010 organizing committee on or before the application deadline. Late applications and materials will not be considered.
  5. Successful applicants will be determined by the paper and the statement.
  6. Stipend grants will be determined by comparative assessment among applicants.
  7. Grants will be issued on site of Pairing 2010.
